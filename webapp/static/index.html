Ichimoku Cloud Strategy Guide

Bullish Signals (BUY)
  • Price above cloud: Strong uptrend confirmed
  • TK Cross (Tenkan > Kijun): Momentum shift upward
  • Green cloud ahead: Future support is bullish
  • Chikou above price: Current strength vs 26 days ago
Bearish Signals (SELL/AVOID)
  • Price below cloud: Strong downtrend
  • TK Cross (Tenkan < Kijun): Momentum fading
  • Red cloud ahead: Future resistance is bearish
  • Chikou below price: Weakness vs 26 days ago
Neutral/Caution

Price inside the cloud = consolidation zone. Wait for breakout before acting. Thin clouds are easier to break; thick clouds provide stronger support/resistance.
